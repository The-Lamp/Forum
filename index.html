Somewhat a Philosopher...

I believe we all have those moments when thoughts in our minds keep appearing and appearing and have to have a way to be shared. Everyone is a philosopher at some point.

I like to think about random things. Sometimes I may come to weird or exciting conclusions.

One day I started writing them down and called it "The Philosopher's Diary." I am not claiming to be a source of truth and somebody might not agree with my point of view. But I am okay with sharing it...
